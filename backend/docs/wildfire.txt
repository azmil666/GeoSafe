## WILDFIRE GUIDANCE
1. PREPARE: Create a defensible space by clearing dry vegetation 30 feet around your home.
2. ALERT: Watch for smoke and listen to emergency alerts. If ordered to evacuate, leave immediately.
3. PROTECTION: Wear N95 masks to protect lungs from smoke. Wear long sleeves and pants made of natural fibers (cotton/wool).
4. INSIDE: Close all windows, vents, and doors to prevent drafts. Remove flammable drapes.
5. DRIVING: Roll up windows and close air vents. Drive slowly with headlights on.
