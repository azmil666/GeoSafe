## EARTHQUAKE GUIDANCE
1. DROP, COVER, HOLD ON: Drop to your hands and knees. Cover your head and neck with your arms. Hold on to sturdy furniture.
2. INDOORS: Stay inside. Do not run outside or use elevators. Stay away from glass, windows, and heavy furniture.
3. OUTDOORS: Move to a clear area away from buildings, trees, streetlights, and utility wires.
4. DRIVING: Pull over to a clear location, stop, and stay inside with your seatbelt fastened.
5. AFTERSHOCKS: Be prepared for aftershocks. Check for gas leaks and structural damage before re-entering buildings.
